Exploring the Incidents of the Bermuda Triangle

Map Content

The dataset used for this map contains 444 recorded incidents within the Bermuda Triangle region. Each event represents a verified occurrence where ships or aircraft were reported lost or experienced unusual circumstances.

This interactive map allows users to explore the spatial distribution and severity of these incidents.
